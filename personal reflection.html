PERSONAL REFLECTION

"A student with faith intergrity,and wisdom online becomes a light in both the real and digital world"

Faith, integrity, and responsible digital citizenship are important values that guide me every day. Faith helps me stay strong, hopeful, and positive, even when things get hard. It reminds me that I am not alone and that I should always try to do what is good and right. My faith inspires me to treat others with kindness and to be thankful for what I have.

Integrity means being honest and doing the right thing, even when no one is watching. I believe that having integrity builds trust and respect. When I make choices, I try to think about how they will affect others. I want people to know that they can rely on me, and that I stand by my words and actions.

As a student and as a person living in a digital world, I also need to practice responsible digital citizenship. This means using the internet wisely and safely.I should respect others online, avoid sharing false information, and protect my personal details. I also need to think before I click, post, or comment, because what I do online can affect others and can stay there forever.

In my life, I want these three values—faith, integrity, and digital responsibility—to guide my decisions. By living with faith, staying honest, and being careful online, I can grow into a better person and contribute to a safer and kinder community.
